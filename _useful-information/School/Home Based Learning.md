<p>The school will be adopting a floating Home-Based Learning (HBL@BDMS)
day this year. Students will learn from home during HBL@BDMS using their
Personal Learning Device (PLD).</p>
<p>Students will also engage in Student-Initiated Learning (SIL), where they
can explore their interests beyond the curriculum. Secondary 1 students
who have not received their PLD may borrow a device from school if needed.</p>

<p>Important Information:</p>

<br>3. Students will follow their timetable for the day, and complete the
required learning and tasks as assigned by their subject teachers.</p>
