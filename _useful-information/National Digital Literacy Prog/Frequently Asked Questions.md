</td>

<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; If there is sufficient funds in your child’s Edusave account, we
will deduct the amount from Edusave. There is no need for any cash payment</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>F3. Are there subsidies for PR/IS students?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; MOE will support all students including PR/IS students who requires
financial assistance. If the family GHI &lt; $4000 or PCI &lt;$1000, you
may request for the Application for Subsidy for Purchase of PLD form. Please
contact the school directly.
<br>&gt; No child should be deprived of the opportunity to own the device.
If you need assistance, please approach the school or submit a query through
<a href="http://go.gov.sg/bdms-icthelp" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>go.gov.sg/bdms-icthelp</u>
</a>
</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>F4. When do we start paying for the device and where do we pay?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; The school bill will be ready to be distributed to all Sec 1 students
and their parents from 17 February 2021. We will <strong>only send the school bills to parents who need to make payment.</strong>
<br>&gt; You may start to make payment after you receive the school bill.&gt;
If you would like to find out details of your school bill, please call
the school.
<br>&gt; Payment can be made using any of the following methods – GIRO/Cheque/Cash
and other e-payment methods stated in the school bill. If you are paying
by cheque or cash, you will need to come down to our school to make your
payment.</p>

<br>&gt; Parents can still continue to pay cash at the school General Office</p>

<h5><strong>About the Enhanced Device Bundle</strong></h5>
<table style="minWidth: 25px">
<colgroup>
<col>
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<th rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D1. Will there still be warranty after my child graduates from school?
<br>
</p>
</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; For students who purchased the device in 2022, the warranty will
last for three years (until 2024), even if some of the students have graduated
from school. This is a 3 year warranty from Lenovo.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D2. I am worried about the wear and tear of rigorous usage….is there warranty
and service desk in school?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; The Enhanced bundle comes with 3 years warranty and 3 years insurance.
<br>&gt; Students can make use of the fortnightly courier service offered
by the contractor for repairs.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D3. Does the device bundle come with the laptop charger?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; Yes. The laptop will come with a USB C charger.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D4. What other accessories are included along with the Chromebook?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; The device bundle will come with a laptop charger, stylus, wired
mouse and a carrier bag</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D5. What happens if my child lost the chromebook or broke it…. Will she
be able to get replace a new one by school or do the parent need to use
their own money to repair it?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; Should your child lose the device, the contractor will decide whether
a replacement can be made. Meanwhile, school will loan your child a device
to use until a new device is available.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D6. What if a stylus is misplaced or stolen or lost? is it replaceable?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; Yes. It is replaceable. It will cost approximately $30. The contractor
has created a website for parents who has purchased the device under this
program and you can purchase accessories through it. You will need to register
for a new account.
<br>&gt; Note that you can only register for an account after you have collected
and enrolled your PLD.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D7. Is a stylus compulsory? What if my Chromebook doesn’t come with a
stylus, do I need to buy a chromebook with stylus?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; With the implementation of the PLD initiative, our curriculum will
be adjusted to make use of the affordances of the device. The stylus will
be useful when taking notes using the device, and it may be required to
complete some of the tasks during lessons (eg. digital drawing during Art,
making notes on images etc)</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>D8. During the repair time, how will my child learn when he/she has no
device?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; While waiting for the device to be repaired, the school will loan
a device to your child, so that learning will not be compromised.</p>
</td>
</tr>

<h5><strong>Other Enquiries</strong></h5>
<table style="minWidth: 25px">
<colgroup>
<col>
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<th rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E1. Do we need to return the device after my child has graduated ?</p>
</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; The device belongs to you. The school will not collect the device
from you. The DMA will be uninstalled when the student graduates.
<br>
</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E2. Where do the students save their work while using the PLD?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; Teachers use different ICT tools for lessons. Where possible, we
will encourage students to save their working documents into the cloud
(ie. Google Drive) or into the platform they are already using (eg. SLS,
other learning portal). Students can choose to save their work into the
PLD, or their personal thumb drive.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E3. Any plans for going to E-books in future? Question is do we still
have to buy these printed books?.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; We are planning to use e-books in the near future as and when they
are available from the publisher. We will determine the quality of the
e-books before we decide to use them.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E4. How long can students use their Student iCON/MIMS account?.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; The Student iCON/MIMS account will be available as long as students
are still studying in MOE schools (ie. Primary, Secondary and JC). These
accounts will no longer be available to use once the students leave the
school system</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E5. Is OneDrive included as part of the Microsoft Pro Plus package?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; OneDrive is not included as part of the Microsoft Pro Plus package.
Should students need to save their documents, they can save either in the
Google Drive or into their PLD.
<br>&gt; OneDrive will be made available soon to students.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E6. How much disk space does the student have using their Student iCON/MIMS
account?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; Students have unlimited space available for their Google Drive.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>E7. How do we prevent a mix up of chrome book when every student brings
to the class?</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>&gt; We will be giving students a label (name and school) to paste on
their PLD. As the PLD belongs to the student, they can personalise their
PLD.</p>

</tr>
