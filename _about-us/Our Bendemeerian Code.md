<h3><strong>Our Bendemeerian Code</strong></h3>
<p>As true Bendemeerians, we are
<br>A community of Critical Thinkers, Courageous Leaders, and Caring Contributors.
<br>We are anchored in values. We dare to aspire,
<br>Motivate one another, and strengthen our strengths,
<br>To lead and serve with purpose.
<br>We lead with courage, integrity and resilience.
<br>We think critically and embrace change with adaptability.
<br>We respect ourselves and others, contributing to a positive and inclusive
community.
<br>We strive for excellence in all our pursuits.
<br>Together, we live out our school motto -
<br>From Each His Best. To Lead and Serve.</p>
