<p>Bendemeer Secondary School has vacancies for the following canteen stalls:-</p>
<p>(i)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Stall 1 – Chinese Economy Rice (with vegetarian options) or Indian Cuisine (with vegetarian options)</strong>
</p>
<p>(ii)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Stall 3 – Malay Economy Rice</strong>
</p>
<p>We would like to invite applicants to operate the abovementioned stalls
in our school.</p>
<p>If you know of any interested applicants, please refer them to our school.</p>
<p><strong>Closing Date: Friday, 27 February 2026</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Requirements</strong>
</p>
<p>Interested applicants must satisfy the following requirements:</p>
<p>a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Must be a Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent
Resident.</p>
<p>b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Must comply with the Healthy Eating in Schools Programme
Guidelines set by the Health Promotion Board. (<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.hpb.gov.sg/schools/school-programmes/healthy-meals-in-schools-programme</a>).</p>
<p>c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Able to provide good service and quality food
at reasonable prices.</p>
<p>d)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Able to observe a high standard of food and personal
hygiene.</p>
<p>e)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Preferably have some experience in running a school
canteen stall.</p>
<p>f)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Successful applicant is required to apply
for a food stall license from Singapore Food Agency (SFA).</p>
<p>g)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Successful applicant and stall assistant must complete
the Basic Food Hygiene Course conducted by SkillsFuture Singapore’s accredited
training providers and then apply for hawker licence from the Singapore
Food Agency (SFA)</p>
<p><strong>Application Procedure</strong>
</p>
<p>The <a href="/files/2026_Application_for_Canteen_Stall_FormBF7.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">application form</a> must
be completed and submitted to our school’s General Office <strong>by 27 Feb 2026</strong> with
the following documents:</p>
<p>(i)&nbsp;&nbsp; Proposed menu and pricing</p>
<p>(ii)&nbsp; Photocopy of the applicant’s NRIC (front and back)</p>
<p>(iii) Photocopy of relevant certificates such as Basic Food Hygiene, etc.</p>
<p>Only shortlisted candidates will be notified for an interview and food
sampling at our school.</p>
